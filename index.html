Data analysis: swiss vehicle traffic accidents

Visualizations done with D3


Introduction

On this page, we can see visualisations of switzerlands vehicle traffic accident data. On the left barchart, the amount of registred vehicles of a specific year over all 26 cantons are visaulized. On the right barchart, we can see the possibility for an accident per vehicle over all 26 cantons. According of those visualisations, we can see that the amount of vehicles has not an impact to the possibility for an accident per vehicle. It is possible thar other factors like infrastructure or amount of habitants have more impact to the accidents rate.

For example: In 2013, the canton ZH (zurich) has the most amount of registered vehicles. But ZH (zurich) is only at the 10th place in the ranking of possibility of an accident per vehicle. Or in the year 2015, BL (basle) has the highest possibility for an accidents per vehicle. But on the left chart, BL (basle) is only on the 13th place.

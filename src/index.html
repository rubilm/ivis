Data analysis: Swiss vehicle traffic accidents

Visualizations done with D3

On this site you see visualisations of switzerland traffic data. On the left site you see the amount of vehicles of a specific year in the specific canton. On the right site you can see the percentage of traffic accident per vehicle. Because of this visualisation you can see that the amount of traffic accidents is not related to the amount of vehicles per canton.

For example in the year 2013 has the canton zurich the most registered vehicles. But is only on the 10th place in the ranking of the accidents per vehicle. Or in the year 2015 has basle the highest rate of accidents per vehicle. But on the chart of the amount of vehicles the are on the xy place.

If you click on a bar on the right site you get an overview over the amount of accidents and their type

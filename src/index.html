Data analysis: Swiss vehicle traffic accidents

Visualizations done with D3

On this page, we can see visualisations of switzerlands vehicle traffic data. On the left barchart, the amount of reported vehicles of a specific year over all 26 cantons are visaulized. On the right barchart, we can see the percentage of traffic accident per vehicle over all 26 cantons. According of those visualisations, we can see that the amount of vehicles has not an impact to the percentage of accident per vehicle. It is possible thar other factors like infrastructure or amount of habitants has more impact to the accidents ratio.

For example: In 2013, the canton ZH (zurich) has the most amount of registered vehicles. But ZH (zurich) is only at the 10th place in the ranking of percentage of accident per vehicle
